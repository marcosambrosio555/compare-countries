    Welcome to CompareCountries

    The ultimate tool for comparing nations across various metrics, including area, population, GDP, and much more. Whether you're a student, researcher, or simply curious about global statistics, CompareCountries provides an easy-to-use platform for exploring and comparing the vital statistics of countries around the world.

    This tool allows users to quickly and efficiently compare different countries based on multiple criteria. By simply selecting the countries and the metrics you're interested in, you can see side-by-side comparisons that offer insights into how these nations stack up against each other.
